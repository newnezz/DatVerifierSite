What is a DAT file?

DAT Files Explained

DAT files are databases containing verified information about ROMs, including correct filenames, sizes, and checksums. They're created and maintained by preservation groups like No-Intro and Redump.

Collection Integrity

DAT files ensure your ROMs are exact copies of the original games, free from hacks, bad dumps, or corrupted files. This guarantees authentic gameplay and preservation.

RetroAchievements Compatibility

Many retro gaming platforms require verified ROMs for achievements to work. Using DAT-verified ROMs ensures full compatibility with RetroAchievements and other gaming platforms.
